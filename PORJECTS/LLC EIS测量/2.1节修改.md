建议把 2.1 节从“只说明扰动注入原理”改成“局部线性化 + 闭环小信号模型 + PI 带宽/稳定性说明”。现在的问题是：原稿在 Eq. (5) 后直接说 PI 电流环可保证 EIS 扫频范围内的准确跟踪，但实验又做到 2 kHz，这正好触发了审稿人对 PI 正弦跟踪、闭环传递函数、环路带宽和稳定裕度的质疑。 审稿意见中也明确指出：未推导闭环 PFM 小信号传递函数、未分析 PI 电流环稳定性、未提供带宽分析、未建立扰动到输出电流的传递关系。

## 1. Eq. (5) 附近要改的核心逻辑

不要再写成“因为 (\Delta f_n \ll f_{n0})，所以 (i_{EIS}) 与 (\Delta f_n) 线性”。这个说法不够准确。

更合理的说法是：

$$
I_{ac,ref} \ll I_{dc,ref}  
$$

因此系统工作点只在 ((I_{dc0}, f_{n0})) 附近小范围变化，LLC 的电流—频率特性可以在该工作点附近做一阶泰勒展开：

$$
i_o(f_n) \approx I_{dc0}+K_f\tilde f_n  
$$

其中：

$$
\tilde f_n=f_n-f_{n0}  
$$

$$
K_f=\left.\frac{\partial I_o}{\partial f_n}\right|_{f_{n0}}  
$$

如果要更严谨，可以补一句二阶误差：

$$
i_o(f_n)=I_{dc0}+K_f\tilde f_n+\frac{1}{2}K_{ff}\tilde f_n^2+\cdots  
$$

$$
K_{ff}=\left.\frac{\partial^2 I_o}{\partial f_n^2}\right|_{f_{n0}}  
$$

只要满足：

$$
\left|\frac{K_{ff}\Delta f_n}{2K_f}\right|\ll 1  
$$

局部线性化就是合理的。

这能回应审稿人提出的“不是因为 (\Delta f_n) 小于 (f_{n0})，而是因为 (I_{ac,ref}) 相对于 (I_{dc,ref}) 很小，所以 LLC 在局部线性区工作”的意见。

## 2. 建议在 Eq. (5) 后新增闭环小信号模型

建议新增一个小段，标题可以不用单独列，直接放在 Eq. (5) 后。

可直接加入如下英文段落：

```text
It should be noted that (5) represents a local small-signal relationship around the steady-state operating point rather than a global linear dependence between the output current and the switching frequency. Let \(\tilde i_o=i_o-I_{dc0}\) and \(\tilde f_n=f_n-f_{n0}\). Since the injected EIS perturbation is much smaller than the dc charging current, the operating point only moves within a narrow region around \((I_{dc0}, f_{n0})\). Therefore, the output current can be approximated by the first-order Taylor expansion
```

$$
\tilde i_o(s)=G_{if}(s)\tilde f_n(s)  
$$

$$
G_{if}(0)=K_f=\left.\frac{\partial I_o}{\partial f_n}\right|_{f_{n0}}  
$$

然后继续写：

```text
For current-loop analysis, the control polarity is included by defining \(u=-\tilde f_n\), since the output current of the LLC converter usually decreases as the normalized switching frequency increases near the selected operating region. The small-signal plant from the PFM control variable to the output current is then written as
```

$$
G_p(s)=\frac{\tilde i_o(s)}{u(s)}=-G_{if}(s)  
$$

PI 控制器写成：

$$
C_i(s)=K_p+\frac{K_i}{s}  
$$

控制律为：

$$
u(s)=C_i(s)\left[\tilde i_{ref}(s)-\tilde i_o(s)\right]  
$$

由此得到闭环输出电流传递函数：

$$
T_i(s)=\frac{\tilde i_o(s)}{\tilde i_{ref}(s)}  
=\frac{C_i(s)G_p(s)}{1+C_i(s)G_p(s)}  
$$

灵敏度函数：

$$
S_i(s)=\frac{\tilde i_{ref}(s)-\tilde i_o(s)}{\tilde i_{ref}(s)}  
=\frac{1}{1+C_i(s)G_p(s)}  
$$

归一化频率调节量为：

$$
\frac{\tilde f_n(s)}{\tilde i_{ref}(s)}

-\frac{C_i(s)}{1+C_i(s)G_p(s)}  
$$

这几条公式基本就补上了审稿人要求的“闭环 PFM 小信号传递函数”和“扰动到输出电流的传递关系”。

## 3. PI 不能说“无误差跟踪 2 kHz”，要换一种说法

PI 对直流参考可以实现零稳态误差，但对 2 kHz 正弦参考不会零误差。不能写成：

> The PI current loop ensures accurate tracking over the whole EIS range.

建议改成：

```text
Although a PI regulator provides zero steady-state error only for a dc reference, zero tracking error with respect to the sinusoidal reference is not required for impedance extraction in the proposed method. The battery impedance is calculated from the synchronously sampled battery voltage and the actual output current, rather than from the commanded current reference. Therefore, the finite amplitude attenuation and phase lag of the current loop with respect to the reference do not directly bias the extracted impedance, provided that the fundamental current component at the excitation frequency is sufficiently large and the harmonic distortion is limited.
```

然后补一句：

```text
In this work, no resonant compensator or additional feedforward term is used. The PI parameters are selected such that the closed-loop bandwidth is higher than the maximum EIS excitation frequency, and the loop stability margin is maintained over the selected operating range.
```

如果你们实际用了前馈，就把最后一句改成：

```text
A frequency-feedforward term based on the local gain \(K_f\) is additionally used to reduce the burden of the PI regulator.
```

对应前馈公式可以写：

$$
u_{ff}(s)=\frac{\tilde i_{ref}(s)}{G_p(0)}  
$$

但如果实验里没做前馈，不要加这句。

## 5. “0.9fr 到 1.1fr”不能只作为实验观察，要写成设计约束

原稿中说 Fig. 8 观察到开关频率被限制在 (0.9f_r) 到 (1.1f_r)，但审稿人会认为这是实验现象，不是理论保证。

建议把它改成：

$$
\Delta f_{n,\max}

\max_{\omega\in \Omega_{EIS}}  
\left|  
\frac{C_i(j\omega)}  
{1+C_i(j\omega)G_p(j\omega)}  
\right|  
I_{ac,ref}  
$$

其中：

$$
\Omega_{EIS}=[2\pi\cdot0.1,\ 2\pi\cdot2000]  
$$

为了保证 LLC 工作在谐振附近，需要满足：

$$
f_{n0}-\Delta f_{n,\max}\ge 0.9  
$$

$$
f_{n0}+\Delta f_{n,\max}\le 1.1  
$$

这样，“频率限制在 (0.9f_r\sim1.1f_r)”就从实验观察变成了控制设计条件。

## 7. 2.1 节可直接替换的整段英文

下面这段可以放在 Eq. (5) 后，作为主要修改内容：

```text
It should be emphasized that (5) is a local small-signal approximation around the steady-state operating point rather than a global linear relationship between the output current and the normalized switching frequency. Let \(\tilde i_o=i_o-I_{dc0}\) and \(\tilde f_n=f_n-f_{n0}\). Since the injected EIS perturbation is much smaller than the dc charging current, the operating point only varies within a narrow region around \((I_{dc0}, f_{n0})\). Therefore, the output current can be linearized as
```

$$
\tilde i_o(s)=G_{if}(s)\tilde f_n(s)  
$$

$$
G_{if}(0)=K_f=\left.\frac{\partial I_o}{\partial f_n}\right|_{f_{n0}}  
$$

```text
For the selected operating region of the LLC converter, the output current decreases as the normalized switching frequency increases. Hence, the control polarity is included by defining \(u=-\tilde f_n\), and the small-signal plant from the PFM control variable to the output current is expressed as
```

$$
G_p(s)=\frac{\tilde i_o(s)}{u(s)}=-G_{if}(s)  
$$

```text
The PI current regulator is given by
```

$$
C_i(s)=K_p+\frac{K_i}{s}  
$$

```text
and the small-signal control law is
```

$$
u(s)=C_i(s)\left[\tilde i_{ref}(s)-\tilde i_o(s)\right]  
$$

```text
Accordingly, the closed-loop transfer function from the injected current reference to the actual output current is
```

$$
T_i(s)=\frac{\tilde i_o(s)}{\tilde i_{ref}(s)}  
=\frac{C_i(s)G_p(s)}{1+C_i(s)G_p(s)}  
$$

```text
and the corresponding sensitivity function is
```

$$
S_i(s)=\frac{1}{1+C_i(s)G_p(s)}  
$$

```text
The normalized switching-frequency variation caused by the injected perturbation is
```

$$
\frac{\tilde f_n(s)}{\tilde i_{ref}(s)}

-\frac{C_i(s)}{1+C_i(s)G_p(s)}  
$$

```text
The loop gain is defined as \(L(s)=C_i(s)G_p(s)\). The PI parameters are selected such that the current-loop crossover frequency is higher than the maximum EIS frequency while maintaining sufficient phase and gain margins. Although a PI regulator cannot provide zero steady-state error for a sinusoidal reference at finite frequency, zero tracking error with respect to the reference is not required for impedance extraction, because the proposed method calculates the impedance using the synchronously sampled battery voltage and actual battery current. The finite amplitude attenuation and phase lag of the current loop only affect the realized perturbation waveform, and their influence is quantified by the closed-loop response \(T_i(j\omega)\), the sensitivity \(S_i(j\omega)\), and the measured current THD.
```

```text
To ensure that the LLC converter remains in the desired resonant operating region during EIS injection, the maximum normalized-frequency deviation is constrained by
```

$$
\Delta f_{n,\max}

\max_{\omega\in \Omega_{EIS}}  
\left|  
\frac{C_i(j\omega)}  
{1+C_i(j\omega)G_p(j\omega)}  
\right|  
I_{ac,ref}  
$$

$$
f_{n0}-\Delta f_{n,\max}\ge 0.9,\qquad  
f_{n0}+\Delta f_{n,\max}\le 1.1  
$$

```text
where \(\Omega_{EIS}\) denotes the EIS excitation-frequency range. This condition confines the switching frequency around the resonant frequency and prevents the perturbation injection from driving the converter into an undesired operating region.
```

## 8. 审稿回复可以这样写

```text
The manuscript has been revised to clarify that Eq. (5) is a local small-signal approximation caused by the small injected current perturbation, rather than a global linear relation only justified by \(\Delta f_n \ll f_{n0}\). A closed-loop small-signal model of the PFM-controlled current loop has been added. The transfer functions from the injected current reference to the output current and to the normalized switching-frequency variation are derived. In addition, the loop gain, bandwidth, phase margin, gain margin, and the 2 kHz tracking performance are now provided. We also clarify that no zero tracking error for the sinusoidal reference is assumed; the impedance is extracted from the measured voltage and current fundamental components, so the finite PI tracking error with respect to the reference does not directly bias the EIS result.
```

核心修改点：不要把 PI 描述成“天然能精确跟踪 2 kHz 正弦”，而是说明“PI 有有限带宽，但带宽和裕度经过设计；EIS 用实测电流和电压提取基波阻抗，因此参考跟踪误差不是主要误差源”。这比简单强化原句更容易说服审稿人。
